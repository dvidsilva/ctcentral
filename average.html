Understanding your brainwaves

Delta (0-4 Hz) Delta waves are associated with very deep relaxation.

Theta (4-7 Hz) Theta waves occur during sleep or deep relaxation.

Alpha (7-13 Hz) Alpha waves occur when we are in a state of wakeful relaxation with closed eyes.

Beta (13-30 Hz) Beta waves are emitted when we are consciously alert, or we feel agitated, tense, afraid.

Gamma (30-70 Hz) Gamma waves are involved in cognitive processing and sudden insight.
