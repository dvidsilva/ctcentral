Delta (0-4 Hz)
Delta waves are the slowest types of brainwaves. They are associated with unconsciousness, deep sleep or catalepsy.
Theta (4-7 Hz)
a state associated with reduced consciousness.
Alpha (7-13 Hz)
Alpha waves occur when we are in a state of physical and mental relaxation, although aware of what is happening around us.
Beta (13-30 Hz)
Beta waves are emitted when we are consciously alert, or we feel agitated, tense, afraid.
Gamma (30-70 Hz)
Gamma waves are involved in cognitive processing,consolidation of information and sudden insight.
